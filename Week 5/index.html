Working with Margins and Padding

Margins are used when doing type setting and is considered the space between the element and any element around it.

Padding is inside the margins. Its considered the space inside the box that will push text or other elements in from the sides.

Customizing the margins and padding

To make an indent on the left side use padding-left in Style.css.You can also add viewwidth (vw) for a responsive page...
